Donation and membership

The Wet Lab is a not-for-profit community organized and operated lab. We are completely donation, volunteer and membership based. The only way we can survive and thrive is if our community supports the lab. All of our equipment has been donated by lab members and participants in the broader DIYBio community. Labs around the globe help each other with materials, reagents, biological parts, equipment, protocols and knowledge.

Please help us survive and thrive by donating money below or signing up for our monthly membership. We need lots of help. Email us at sdwetlab@gmail.com if you have any questions or want to help out in any way.

Donation: We ask for $5/month donation for anyone who comes, visits and participates.
